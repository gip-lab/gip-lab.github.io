Twin Accord: The Lost Opera

A twist of the Cinderella story where the happy ever after isn't as happy as you may think. In this action game, you play as Sandy (Cinderella) escaping the castle which has now become just another prison to her. You must fight the royal rats, who were cursed by Queen Julia to do household chores like cleaning and cooking, but also to stop Sandy from escaping.

This is the project that I am currently working on with my third year team at university. I am one of three programmers, my focus being on the player characters themselves in both movement and combat. The github is inaccessable due to it being on the internal university domain. There is, however, a public itch.io to access.
