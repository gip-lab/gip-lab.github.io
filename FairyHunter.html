Fairy Hunter

A game prototype I made as my first year game development course project. It's a turn-based RPG about a boy who upsets a fairy princess, who then turns his brother into a frog as revenge.

>NOTE: The game comes as a zip file, it must be unzipped before it is played. The download link is in the download section of this page, it will link to a dropbox page with the game on it.
